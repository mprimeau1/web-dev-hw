Spinich & Pea Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 1 Cup spinach
  • 7.5 oz canned green lentils (drained and rinsed)
  • 3/4 Cup oatmeal (dry)
  • 1 TBSP chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Defrost peas
  2. Take the dry oatmeal and buzz it in a food processor until fine like flour.
  3. Add the peas, spinach, lentils, onion, parsley and garlic into the food processor. Blend until coarsely blended.
  4. Shape into burger shapes. They hold together better if you freeze them first, but if you want them now freeze for at least 30 minutes before cooking.
  5. Pre-heat oven to 350oF. Place the burgers evenly apart on a baking tray. Make sure to oil the pan a little. Bake for 30 minutes total, flipping half way.
  6. Build burger however you want!
