Make Bread The Easy Way

No Kneed Bread

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of flour
  • 3/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 1/2 tst sea salt
  • 3 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 TBSP of your choice seasoning (I suggest garlic and rosemary or rasin and cinnamon)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl mix flour,yeast, salt and seasonings.
  2. Add warm water and combine. The dough should be really wet (if not add more water)
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and a dish towel and wait 3 hours for it to rise
  4. The dough will be dotted with holes/bubbles. You can now divide the dough into two
  5. Turn each half of dough into a well floured surface. Tuck its edges in to fold into a loaf shape
  6. Lay each loaf into a pan(cast iron dutch oven) with parchment paper underneath
  7. Let the dough rise another 1/2 hour
  8. Set your oven to 450 degrees f. Place on the bottom row of the oven a baking pan filled with water to steam the oven
  9. Put bread into oven with dutch oven lid on for 30 minutes
  10. Remove lid and bake for 15-20 minutes (knock on the bread if it sounds hollow then its ready)
  11. Let cool for 1 hour before cutting
